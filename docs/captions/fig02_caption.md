# Figure 2 — Worker hours and mobility (CPS)

**Panel A** plots weighted mean usual weekly hours by month and AI-relevance tercile for employed workers in the monthly CPS. **Panel B** summarizes matched-month transition flows: a heatmap of weighted transition counts for the latest month in the series and line charts of summary transition metrics (retention, occupation switching, unemployment entry, NILF entry) over time.

Alt-text: Line chart of hours by AI tercile over time; heatmap of transition counts and small multiples of transition rates.
