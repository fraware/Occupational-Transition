# Figure 4 — Labor demand and payroll context (JOLTS and CES)

**What is plotted.** **Panel A** reports JOLTS sector rates over time for openings, hires, quits, and layoffs/discharges across the paper's six-sector comparison set. **Panel B** reports CES payroll employment for the same sectors, indexed to 100 in August 2023.

**How to read it.** In Panel A, compare within-sector movement over time and cross-sector level differences for each flow rate. In Panel B, values above 100 indicate payroll employment above the August 2023 baseline. Values below 100 indicate levels below baseline.

**Interpretation.** The figure provides sector-level labor-demand and payroll context for the worker-side and firm-side descriptive evidence in the main text.

**Boundary.** These series are sector aggregates and do not identify occupation-level demand flows, AI-attributed demand, or causal AI effects.

Alt-text: Two-panel figure. Panel A is a set of multi-line JOLTS rate charts over time for openings, hires, quits, and layoffs/discharges by sector group. Panel B is a multi-line CES payroll-employment index chart for the same sectors, with August 2023 fixed at 100.
