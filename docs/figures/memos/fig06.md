# Figure 6 memo — Survey-design roadmap

## Question

If the public system is modular rather than integrated, what is the most defensible sequence of survey and publication extensions?

## Main takeaway

Survey design should follow the observability frontier. The point is not to demand an ideal linked system immediately, nor to pretend that the current stack is sufficient. It is to strengthen exactly the margins where informational returns are highest.

## Design objective

Figure 6 should make the policy logic feel structurally derived from the paper’s empirical diagnosis. The reader should see that the recommendations are not free-floating proposals: they are consequences of the capability map and the missing worker-firm AI linkage.

## Constraints

This is a synthesis figure, not an estimate. It should remain cleaner and more diagrammatic than Figures 1–5, while still obeying the repo’s publication-figure contract.
