# Figure 4 — Labor-demand and payroll context (JOLTS and CES)

**What is plotted.** **Panel A** reports JOLTS sector rates over time for openings, hires, quits, and layoffs/discharges across the paper’s six-sector comparison set. **Panel B** reports CES payroll employment for the same sectors, indexed to 100 in August 2023.

**How to read it.** In Panel A, compare within-sector movement over time and cross-sector level differences separately for each JOLTS flow rate rather than collapsing them into a single demand indicator. In Panel B, values above 100 indicate payroll employment above the August 2023 baseline, while values below 100 indicate payroll employment below that benchmark.

**Interpretation.** The figure provides official sector-level demand and payroll context for the worker-side and business-side descriptive evidence shown elsewhere in the paper. It is intentionally contextual rather than causal.

**Boundary.** These series are sector aggregates. They do not identify occupation-level demand flows, AI-specific labor demand, or causal effects of AI adoption on sector outcomes.
